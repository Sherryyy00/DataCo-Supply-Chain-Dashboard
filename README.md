📌 Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end Business Intelligence solution developed using Power BI, focused on analysing an e-commerce dataset to evaluate sales performance, profitability, customer behaviour, and key business drivers.

The dashboard is designed to support data-driven decision-making by transforming raw transactional data into structured, interactive, and insight-driven visualisations.

🎯 Objectives
Analyse overall business performance using key KPIs
Identify sales trends and growth patterns over time
Evaluate the impact of discounts on profitability
Understand product and customer contribution to revenue
Highlight regional performance and concentration

🧩 Key Features

📊 KPI Dashboard
Total Sales
Total Profit
Profit Margin (%)
Average Order Value (AOV)

📈 Sales & Trend Analysis
Time-series analysis of revenue performance
Comparative analysis of sales and profit trends

🔍 Sales Drivers Analysis
Decomposition Tree to identify key contributors to revenue
Drill-down by product category, region, and customer attributes

📦 Product Analysis
Pareto Analysis (Cumulative Sales %)
Product contribution to total revenue
Identification of high-performing and low-performing products

👥 Customer Analysis
Customer ranking based on revenue contribution
Analysis of customer concentration and behaviour patterns

🌍 Regional Analysis
Sales comparison across different regions
Identification of high-performing markets

🎥 Animated Visualisation
Time-based animation using play axis
Demonstrates progression of sales performance over years

🛠️ Tools & Technologies
Power BI Desktop
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
Power Query (Data Transformation)
Data Modelling (Star Schema approach)

🧠 Key Insights
Revenue is concentrated within a limited number of product categories and regions
Discount strategies influence both sales volume and profitability
Customer contribution is uneven, with a small segment driving a large share of revenue
Sales trends remain relatively stable, with moderate fluctuations over time

📚 Learning Outcomes
Through this project, I developed practical experience in:
End-to-end BI workflow implementation
KPI design and performance evaluation
Advanced DAX calculations
Selecting appropriate visuals for analytical storytelling
Building interactive and user-focused dashboards

🚀 Future Improvements
